ChildCare

Every child, no matter who they are or where they come from, deserves access to education.



ChildCare is a social service non-profit organiazation providing right education to the poor and needy children.
We have devised several solutions that help us continue sharing the gift of education with underprivileged children.
Therefore, to ensure education for all, our campaign ‘ChildCare’ aims not only at bringing drop out children back
to school but also in enabling uninterrupted learning for children through access to resources needed for their education.
